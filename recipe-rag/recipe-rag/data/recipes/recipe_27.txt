Title: Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, combine the melted butter and granulated sugar. Whisk well.
3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth.
4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Stir with a spatula until just combined.
6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
7. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread evenly.
8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs (not wet batter).
9. Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before cutting.
