Title: Classic Potato Salad

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4 large eggs, hard-boiled
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
- 1 medium celery stalk, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:
1. Place the cubed potatoes in a large pot. Cover with cold water by 1 inch. Add the 1 teaspoon of salt.
2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10-12 minutes, until just fork-tender. Drain and let cool.
3. Peel and chop the hard-boiled eggs.
4. In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and pickle relish.
5. Add the cooled potatoes, chopped eggs, celery, and red onion to the bowl.
6. Gently fold everything together with a rubber spatula until the potatoes are evenly coated.
7. Season with black pepper and additional salt to taste.
8. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to let the flavors meld.
