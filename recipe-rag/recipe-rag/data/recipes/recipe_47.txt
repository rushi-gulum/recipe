Title: Simple Fruit Salad

Ingredients:
- 1 cup strawberries, hulled and quartered
- 1 cup green grapes, halved
- 1 cup blueberries
- 1 (15 oz) can mandarin oranges, drained
- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine the strawberries, grapes, blueberries, and drained mandarin oranges.
2. In a small bowl, toss the sliced banana with the lemon juice (this prevents browning).
3. Add the banana slices to the large bowl.
4. If using, drizzle the honey over the fruit.
5. Toss gently to combine.
6. Serve immediately or chill for 30 minutes.
