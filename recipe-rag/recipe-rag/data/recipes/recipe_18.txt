Title: Simple Baked Salmon

Ingredients:
- 2 (6 oz) salmon fillets, skin on or off
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 2 sprigs fresh dill (or 1/2 teaspoon dried)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
3. Pat the salmon dry with a paper towel.
4. Drizzle the olive oil over both fillets, and rub to coat.
5. Season generously with salt and pepper.
6. Top each fillet with lemon slices and a sprig of dill.
7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
8. Serve immediately.
