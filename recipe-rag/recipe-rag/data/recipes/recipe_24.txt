Title: Basic Tomato Bisque

Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (28 oz) can whole peeled tomatoes, with juices
- 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
2. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
3. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add the can of whole tomatoes (with their juices), vegetable broth, basil, salt, and pepper.
5. Use a spoon to break up the tomatoes.
6. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat, cover, and cook for 20 minutes.
7. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, let it cool slightly and blend in batches in a regular blender.
8. Return the soup to the pot. Stir in the heavy cream.
9. Heat gently over low heat for 5 minutes. Do not let it boil.
10. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot.
