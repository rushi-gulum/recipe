Title: Quick Tuna Salad

Ingredients:
- 2 (5 oz) cans of tuna, drained
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a medium bowl, flake the drained tuna with a fork.
2. Add the mayonnaise, celery, red onion, relish, and Dijon mustard.
3. Stir well until everything is evenly combined.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Serve on sandwiches, with crackers, or over a bed of lettuce.
