Title: Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 medium carrots, sliced
- 2 celery stalks, sliced
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 cups egg noodles, uncooked
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
2. Add the onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
3. Add the chicken broth, thyme, and bay leaf. Bring to a simmer.
4. Carefully add the whole chicken breasts to the pot.
5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
6. Remove the chicken breasts and place them on a cutting board. Shred the chicken using two forks.
7. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
8. Add the uncooked egg noodles and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.
9. Remove the bay leaf.
10. Stir in the fresh parsley and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
