Title: Quick Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:
- 8 oz linguine pasta
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/2 cup dry white wine (or chicken broth)
- 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook the linguine according to package directions. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
2. In a large skillet, melt the butter with the olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add the shrimp to the skillet. Cook for 1-2 minutes per side, until pink and cooked through. Do not overcook.
5. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
6. Add the white wine (or broth) and lemon juice to the skillet. Bring to a simmer and let it reduce by half, about 2-3 minutes.
7. Return the cooked pasta and the cooked shrimp to the skillet.
8. Add the chopped parsley and toss everything to combine.
9. If the pasta is too dry, add a splash of the reserved pasta water.
10. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
