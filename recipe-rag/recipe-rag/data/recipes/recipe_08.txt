Title: Roasted Root Vegetables

Ingredients:
- 1 lb (450g) carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 lb (450g) parsnips, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 large sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1 large red onion, cut into wedges
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. On a large, rimmed baking sheet, combine the carrots, parsnips, sweet potato, and red onion.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
4. Drizzle the oil and herb mixture over the vegetables.
5. Use your hands to toss the vegetables, ensuring they are all evenly coated.
6. Spread the vegetables in a single, even layer on the baking sheet.
7. Roast for 35-45 minutes, tossing halfway through, until the vegetables are tender and caramelized at the edges.
8. Serve warm as a side dish.
