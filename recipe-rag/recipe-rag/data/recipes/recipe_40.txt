Title: Simple Spinach Salad

Ingredients:
- 6 oz fresh baby spinach
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries
- 1/2 cup candied pecans (or walnuts)
- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup crumbled feta or goat cheese
- Balsamic vinaigrette dressing

Instructions:
1. In a large salad bowl, add the baby spinach.
2. Top the spinach with the dried cranberries, candied pecans, sliced red onion, and crumbled feta cheese.
3. Just before serving, drizzle with your desired amount of balsamic vinaigrette.
4. Toss gently to combine or serve as is, letting guests dress their own portions.
