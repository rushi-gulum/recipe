Title: Simple Fried Rice

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
- 3 cups cold, leftover cooked rice
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:
1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
2. Add the onion, peas, and carrots. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until tender-crisp.
3. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil to the empty side.
5. Pour the beaten eggs onto the empty side. Scramble them until cooked, then break them up and mix with the vegetables.
6. Add the cold, leftover rice to the skillet. Break it up with your spoon.
7. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes, until the rice is heated through.
8. Drizzle the soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice. Stir well to combine everything.
9. Serve hot.
