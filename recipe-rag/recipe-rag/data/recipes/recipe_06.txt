Title: Quick Chicken Quesadillas

Ingredients:
- 4 large flour tortillas
- 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
- 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
- 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1/2 cup corn, frozen or canned
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter

Instructions:
1. In a medium bowl, combine the shredded cooked chicken, black beans, corn, chili powder, and cumin. Mix well.
2. Lay one tortilla flat on a clean surface. Sprinkle about 1/4 of the cheese mixture over one half of the tortilla.
3. Top the cheese with 1/4 of the chicken and bean mixture.
4. Sprinkle another 1/4 of the cheese on top of the chicken mixture.
5. Fold the other half of the tortilla over the filling.
6. Heat the olive oil or butter in a large skillet or griddle over medium heat.
7. Carefully place the folded tortilla onto the skillet.
8. Cook for 2-4 minutes per side, or until the tortilla is golden brown and crispy and the cheese is melted.
9. Remove from the skillet and cut into wedges.
10. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling. Serve with sour cream and salsa.
