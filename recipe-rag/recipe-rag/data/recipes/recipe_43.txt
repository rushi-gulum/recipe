Title: Simple Caprese Salad

Ingredients:
- 2 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. On a large platter, alternate and overlap the slices of tomato, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves.
2. Drizzle the entire salad with the extra virgin olive oil.
3. Drizzle with the balsamic glaze.
4. Sprinkle lightly with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
5. Serve immediately.
