Title: Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and quartered
- 4 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup whole milk
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 tablespoons chives, chopped (optional)

Instructions:
1. Place the quartered potatoes and whole garlic cloves in a large pot.
2. Add cold water to cover the potatoes by at least 1 inch. Add the 1 teaspoon of salt.
3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are very tender.
4. While potatoes are cooking, warm the milk and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until the butter is melted. Do not boil.
5. Drain the potatoes and garlic well. Return them to the hot pot.
6. Mash the potatoes and garlic with a potato masher.
7. Pour the warm milk and butter mixture over the potatoes.
8. Continue mashing or switch to a whisk to make them fluffy.
9. Stir in the black pepper and optional chives.
10. Taste and add more salt if needed. Serve hot.
