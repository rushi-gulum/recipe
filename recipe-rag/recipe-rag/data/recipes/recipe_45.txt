Title: Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:
- 1 (12-inch) pre-baked pizza crust (or fresh dough)
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
- 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
- 1/4 cup green bell pepper, chopped (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Place the pizza crust on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border.
4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
5. Arrange the pepperoni slices and any other toppings (olives, peppers) on top of the cheese.
6. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
