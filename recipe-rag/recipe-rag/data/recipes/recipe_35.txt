Title: Classic Oatmeal

Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup rolled oats (old-fashioned)
- 1 cup water (or milk)
- 1 pinch of salt
- Toppings: brown sugar, berries, nuts, maple syrup

Instructions:
1. In a small saucepan, combine the rolled oats, water (or milk), and salt.
2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer.
4. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oatmeal has thickened to your liking.
5. Pour the oatmeal into a bowl.
6. Add your favorite toppings, such as a spoonful of brown sugar, fresh berries, or a drizzle of maple syrup.
