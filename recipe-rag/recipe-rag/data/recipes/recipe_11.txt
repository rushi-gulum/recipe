Title: Classic Guacamole

Ingredients:
- 3 ripe avocados
- 1 lime, juiced
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
- 1 jalapeno, minced (optional)
- 1/2 roma tomato, diced

Instructions:
1. Slice the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a medium bowl.
2. Mash the avocado with a fork to your desired consistency.
3. Immediately add the lime juice and salt. Stir to combine.
4. Add the red onion, cilantro, jalapeno (if using), and tomato.
5. Stir gently until everything is well mixed.
6. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary (more salt or lime juice).
7. Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
