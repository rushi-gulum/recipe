Title: Simple Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lbs ground lamb (or ground beef)
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and quartered (for topping)
- 1/4 cup milk (for topping)
- 2 tablespoons butter (for topping)

Instructions:
1. Make topping: Boil potatoes until tender. Drain, return to pot. Mash with milk and butter. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
2. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
3. In a large skillet, brown the ground lamb over medium-high heat. Drain fat.
4. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened (8-10 minutes).
5. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook for 1 minute.
6. Stir in the beef broth and rosemary. Bring to a simmer, then cook for 5 minutes until it starts to thicken.
7. Stir in the frozen peas and corn.
8. Pour the meat and vegetable mixture into a 9x13 baking dish.
9. Spread the mashed potato topping evenly over the filling.
10. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
11. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
