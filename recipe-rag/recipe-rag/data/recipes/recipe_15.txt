Title: Simple Beef Tacos

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup water
- 8-10 hard or soft taco shells
- Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream

Instructions:
1. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat until browned, breaking it apart. Drain excess fat.
2. Reduce heat to medium. Add the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt. Stir to coat the beef.
3. Cook for 1 minute until the spices are fragrant.
4. Add the water to the skillet, stir, and bring to a simmer.
5. Let it simmer for 3-5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
6. Warm the taco shells according to package directions.
7. Fill each taco shell with the seasoned beef mixture.
8. Top with your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
