Title: Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1/4 cup soy sauce, divided
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 large head of broccoli, cut into florets
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
- 1/4 cup chicken broth
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:
1. In a medium bowl, combine the chicken pieces, 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, and the cornstarch. Toss to coat and set aside for 10 minutes.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining 3 tablespoons of soy sauce, chicken broth, and sesame oil. This is your sauce.
3. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
4. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove chicken to a plate.
5. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil to the skillet.
6. Add the broccoli florets and onion. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until broccoli is bright green.
7. Add the garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
8. Return the chicken to the skillet.
9. Pour the sauce mixture over everything. Stir and cook for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
10. Serve immediately over rice.
