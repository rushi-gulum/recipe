Title: Roasted Asparagus

Ingredients:
- 1 lb fresh asparagus, tough ends trimmed
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 lemon

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Place the trimmed asparagus on a large baking sheet.
3. Drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Toss with your hands to coat the spears evenly.
5. Arrange the asparagus in a single layer.
6. Roast for 10-15 minutes, depending on thickness, until tender-crisp.
7. Remove from the oven and squeeze the juice of half a lemon over the top.
8. Serve immediately.
