Title: Easy Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into small cubes
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 packet (1 oz) taco seasoning mix
- 1/4 cup water
- 8-10 small flour or corn tortillas
- Toppings: salsa, sour cream, shredded lettuce, lime wedges

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the cubed chicken. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned and cooked through.
3. Sprinkle the taco seasoning mix over the chicken. Stir to coat.
4. Add the 1/4 cup of water. Stir and bring to a simmer.
5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
6. Warm the tortillas in the microwave or a dry skillet.
7. Fill the warm tortillas with the chicken mixture and your favorite toppings.
