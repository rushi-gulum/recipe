Title: Simple Green Beans

Ingredients:
- 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 cup water

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the sliced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until golden and fragrant. Do not let it burn.
3. Add the trimmed green beans to the skillet. Toss to coat in the oil and garlic.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3-4 minutes.
6. Carefully add the 1/4 cup of water to the skillet (it will steam).
7. Cover the skillet, reduce heat to medium-low, and let the beans steam for 3-5 minutes, or until tender-crisp.
8. Uncover, increase heat to medium-high, and cook for 1-2 minutes more, until any remaining water has evaporated.
9. Serve hot.
