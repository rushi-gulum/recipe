Title: Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:
- 1 cup strawberries, frozen
- 1 ripe banana
- 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt (or Greek yogurt)
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)

Instructions:
1. Add all ingredients to a blender: frozen strawberries, banana, milk, and yogurt.
2. Add the optional honey or maple syrup if you prefer a sweeter smoothie.
3. Secure the lid and blend on high for 45-60 seconds, or until the smoothie is thick and creamy.
4. If the smoothie is too thick, add a splash more milk and blend again.
5. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
