Title: Simple Baked Ziti

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ziti or penne pasta
- 1 lb Italian sausage (or ground beef)
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (28 oz) jar marinara sauce
- 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cook the pasta according to package directions, but cook for 2 minutes less than the al dente time. Drain.
3. While pasta cooks, brown the Italian sausage and onion in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Drain fat.
4. Stir the marinara sauce into the skillet with the sausage. Simmer for 5 minutes.
5. In a medium bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and the beaten egg. Season with salt and pepper.
6. In a large 9x13 baking dish, spread 1 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom.
7. Top with half of the cooked pasta.
8. Dollop half of the ricotta mixture over the pasta.
9. Top with half of the remaining meat sauce and 1 cup of mozzarella.
10. Repeat the layers: remaining pasta, remaining ricotta, remaining meat sauce.
11. Sprinkle the final 1 cup of mozzarella cheese on top.
12. Bake, uncovered, for 25-30 minutes, until bubbly and the cheese is golden. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
