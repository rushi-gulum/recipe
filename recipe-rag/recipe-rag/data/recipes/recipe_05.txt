Title: Hearty Lentil Soup

Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 medium carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup brown or green lentils, rinsed
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
2. Add the onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, about 8-10 minutes.
3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
4. Stir in the rinsed lentils, vegetable broth, thyme, cumin, and bay leaf.
5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
6. Remove the bay leaf.
7. Season the soup with salt and black pepper to taste.
8. Stir in the optional lemon juice just before serving to brighten the flavors.
9. Serve hot, with a side of crusty bread.
