Title: Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients:
- 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 large head of broccoli, cut into florets
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
- 1/4 cup beef broth
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, toss the sliced flank steak with 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and the cornstarch. Set aside for 15 minutes.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining 2 tablespoons soy sauce, beef broth, and oyster sauce (if using).
3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
4. Add the beef in a single layer (work in batches) and cook for 1-2 minutes per side until browned. Remove beef to a plate.
5. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet, along with 2 tablespoons of water. Cover and steam for 2-3 minutes.
6. Uncover, add the garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for 1 minute.
7. Return the beef to the skillet.
8. Pour the sauce mixture over the beef and broccoli. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
9. Stir in the sesame oil. Serve immediately with rice.
