Title: Roasted Chicken Thighs

Ingredients:
- 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400째F (200째C).
2. Pat the chicken thighs completely dry with paper towels.
3. In a small bowl, combine the salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, and paprika.
4. Place the chicken thighs in a large bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat.
5. Sprinkle the spice mixture over the chicken and rub it in, making sure to get it under the skin as well.
6. Arrange the chicken thighs, skin-side up, in a single layer on a rimmed baking sheet or in a roasting pan.
7. Roast for 35-45 minutes, or until the skin is golden and crispy and the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C).
8. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
